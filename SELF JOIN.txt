Q1.WAQTD NAME OF THE EMPLOYEE AND HIS MANAGER'S NAME IF EMPLOYEE IS WORKING AS CLERK ?
ANS:-

QUERY:
SELECT E1.ENAME,E2.ENAME
FROM EMP E1,EMP E2
WHERE E1.MGR=E2.EMPNO AND E1.JOB='CLERK';

OUTPUT:
ENAME      ENAME
---------- ----------
SMITH      FORD
ADAMS      SCOTT
JAMES      BLAKE
MILLER     CLARK

Q10.WAQTD EMP NAME AND MANAGER NAME ALONG WITH THEIR ANNUAL SALARIES IF EMPLOYEE WORKS IN DEPT 10,20 AND MANAGER SAL IS GREATER THAN EMP SALARY?
ANS:-

QUERY:
